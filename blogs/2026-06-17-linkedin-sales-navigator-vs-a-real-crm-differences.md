Sales teams often assume LinkedIn Sales Navigator can replace a CRM, but Sales Navigator helps identify opportunities and start conversations while a CRM organizes those conversations and moves deals forward with full email history, meeting tracking, and pipeline management. The article argues successful teams use both together, Sales Navigator for prospect discovery and a CRM for relationship management.
