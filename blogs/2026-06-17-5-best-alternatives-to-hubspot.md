With HubSpot's steep prices that can be hard to scale, this article reviews five alternatives: Zoho ($14-$52/user/month), Freshsales ($9-$59/user/month), folk ($20-$80/user/month, recommended for 20-50 person teams), Monday CRM ($12-$28/seat/month), and Salesforce ($25-$500/user/month). It cites rising costs, limited customization, scalability limits, and integration gaps as the main reasons teams switch.
