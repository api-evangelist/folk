This guide explains three methods for exporting LinkedIn contacts: LinkedIn's native CSV feature (free but limited), direct CRM integration via the folkX extension (enriched with additional fields), and manual export to Excel. It notes LinkedIn does not provide phone numbers or private emails, but enrichment tools can add verified contact information, and stresses that exported contacts only have value when segmented and integrated into a real sales workflow.
